About Us
Here's what we do...

GigGuru is your guide to finding the perfect gigs for you! We pride ourselves in being the biggest promoter of music around the world - From the Freshest Artists Worldwide to the Biggest Household Names of All Time. Each month we promote gigs of a certain genre and our favourite underrated artists (as well as the popular ones!) within that genre. We also keep everyone up to date with current music events and music festivals. So what are you waiting for? Use GigGuru to grab your tickets to the grooviest Gig n' Go!
